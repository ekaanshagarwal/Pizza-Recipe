PIZZA
INGREDIENTS
FOR PIZZA DOUGH:
▢ ¼ cup curd / yogurt
▢ 1 tsp  sugar
▢ ½ tsp baking powder
▢ ¼ tsp baking powder
▢ ½ tsp salt
▢ 2 tbsp oil
▢ 2 cup maida / plain flour
▢ ¾ cup water

FOR TOPPING:
▢ 4 tsp  pizza sauce
▢ small cubes onion
▢ few sliced capsicum
▢ few sliced olives
▢ 3 slice tomato
▢ few jalapeno, chopped
▢ 1 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
▢ ¼ tsp chilli flakes
▢ ¼ tsp mixed herbs
▢ 1 tsp oil

INSTRUCTIONS
1.further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent. this helps the crust to fluff slightly.
2.then prick with a centre of dough with the help of a fork to prevent pizza base from fluffing up like chapati.
3.spread 4 tsp of pizza sauce uniformly.
4.further top with onion, capsicum, olives, tomato and jalapenos.
5.also, spread 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
6.further, sprinkle ¼ tsp chilli flakes and ¼ tsp mixed herbs.
7.place the rolled pizza base into the oven.
8.finally, slice the pizza and serve hot.
